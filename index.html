My History

I have been a bus driver for almost 3 years. I passed my bus driving license in Leipzig. It was a great adventure for me, but also an opportunity to learn something about myself. I know that I am responsible, people can trust me and I can deal with difficult, stressful situations.

After graduation, I worked as a dietitian. I had a private practice where I helped people with obesity, diabetes and cardiovascular diseases. I dealt with setting menus, modifying the diet depending on the results obtained by patients, and constantly monitoring the progress of my patients. It was a job that required empathy, understanding the needs of others and taking responsibility for their health.

When I came to Germany, I didn't know the language very well, so I had to start with physical work. These were mainly warehouses and factories. It was a difficult time which required patience and learning German in order to have a chance at a better job in the future.
